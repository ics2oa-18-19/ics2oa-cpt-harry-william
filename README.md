# Kungfu_Chef
There will be food dropping from the top of the window, and as the "chef", you have to click on the food to catch them. You have three chances, if the food dropped on the floor, which means that you didn't manage to click on them, you will lose one health. You will lose 5 score everytime you miss-click, and gain 10 score when successfully click on the food. The food will dropping faster and increase quantities as the time progresses . The objective is to survive as much time as possible.
